Lion

Lions are symbols of strength and courage and have been celebrated throughout history for these characteristics.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 150 centimiters
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Forest and South Sahara

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth, and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. Their coats are yellow-gold.
